Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a payment using UPI?

Open any UPI-enabled app, scan the QR code or enter our UPI ID (8389873317@fam), input the amount, and confirm the payment. The transaction is usually instant.

Is there any transaction fee?

No, we don't charge any fees. However, your bank might have transaction limits or charges depending on your account type.

How long does payment processing take?

UPI payments are typically processed instantly. In rare cases, it may take up to 24 hours if there are network issues.

What if my payment fails?

Check your bank account to confirm if the amount was deducted. If deducted but not received by us, contact your bank and share the transaction details with our support team at rohitprasadyadav06@gmail.com.
