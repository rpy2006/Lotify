Installation Guide

  1. Download the APK file using the button above
  2. Open your device's Settings > Security
  3. Enable Unknown Sources to allow installation of apps outside the Play Store
  4. Open the downloaded APK file and tap Install
  5. Once installed, open the app and grant necessary permissions
  6. The app will automatically scan your device for music files
  7. Start enjoying your music collection!

Note: If you encounter any issues during installation, ensure you have sufficient storage space and try again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MusicPlayer free to use?
Yes, MusicPlayer is completely free with no hidden subscriptions or in-app purchases. All features are available without any limitations.
Does the app contain ads?
No, MusicPlayer is ad-free. We believe in providing an uninterrupted music experience.
How do I transfer my playlists from another music player?
You can export playlists as M3U files from most music players and import them into MusicPlayer via the Playlists section.
Does MusicPlayer support streaming services?
Currently, MusicPlayer is designed for local music playback. We may add streaming service integration in future updates.
Can I request new features?
Absolutely! We welcome feature requests through our support page. Popular requests are prioritized for future updates.
